Founded by Riley, a lifelong tea enthusiast, Phinbrew was born from his love for Japanese tea discovered while serving in Japan and Korea with the U.S. Air Force. Inspired by his mother's cancer battle, Riley shares green tea's empowering health benefits. Green tea brings calmness and clarity, like a martial artist's focus.

A Martial Artist's Discipline

"Living in Brussels as a karate brown belt, green tea amplified his focus, echoing Miyamoto Musashi's discipline."

Riley's passion ignited in Japan and Korea, where sencha's grassy notes and Jeoncha's roasted depth captivated him during USAF service. Phinbrew blends this martial arts-inspired clarity with tea's health benefits, offering hand-picked teas, accessories, and incense from trusted farms in Japan, Korea, and China.
