Our Story

Founded by Riley, a lifelong tea enthusiast, Phinbrew was born from his love for Japanese tea discovered while serving in Japan and Korea with the U.S. Air Force.

When Riley began drinking green tea regularly, his life transformed— his skin glowed, energy soared, and focus sharpened—thanks to sencha’s antioxidants, anti-cancer properties, and benefits for skin and gums.

Inspired by his mother’s cancer battle, Riley shares green tea’s empowering health benefits.

Green tea brings calmness and clarity, like a martial artist’s focus. Its catechins fight free radicals, reducing cancer risk and boosting wellness.

Riley's Journey

Riley’s passion ignited in Japan and Korea, where sencha’s grassy notes and Jeoncha’s roasted depth captivated him during USAF service.

Living in Brussels as a karate brown belt, green tea amplified his focus, echoing Miyamoto Musashi's discipline.

Phinbrew blends this martial arts-inspired clarity with tea’s health benefits, offering hand-picked teas, accessories, and incense.

Riley hand-picks from trusted farms in Japan, Korea, and China for authenticity and sustainability—from matcha whisks to coffee grinders.
